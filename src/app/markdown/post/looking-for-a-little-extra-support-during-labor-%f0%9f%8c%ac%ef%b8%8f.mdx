✨ At Heartbeat Midwifery, we offer nitrous oxide as a safe, effective option for pain relief during birth. Commonly known as ‘laughing gas,’ nitrous helps take the edge off contractions while allowing you to stay fully present and in control of your experience.One of the best things about nitrous? You’re in charge! You hold the mask and decide when and how much to use. It works quickly, wears off fast, and won’t affect baby—giving you the flexibility to manage labor on your terms.Birth is all about choices, and we’re here to support yours. 💕👶&nbsp;
