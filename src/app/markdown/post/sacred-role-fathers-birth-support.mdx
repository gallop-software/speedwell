# The Sacred Role of Fathers

<Image
  src="/images/sacred-role.jpg"
  size="large"
  wrap={true}
  alt="The sacred role of fathers in birth and pregnancy support"
/>

Fatherhood is a powerful, transformative journey — one that begins long before the first step outside the birth room.

The science is clear: fathers play an integral role in the health and well-being of their partner, their baby, and themselves.

## Did you know?

**Increased involvement in pregnancy and birth leads to higher levels of oxytocin for both moms and dads**, creating deeper bonding and emotional connections.

**Research shows that fathers who are actively present during birth can reduce the risk of postpartum depression for their partners** by providing emotional support and shared responsibility.

**Studies have found that babies with engaged fathers tend to show stronger cognitive and emotional development**, and have lower rates of behavioral issues as they grow.

But it's not just about the baby or the mother — fathers thrive in this process too.

**Dads who engage emotionally in pregnancy and birth report higher levels of self-esteem, lower levels of stress, and a deeper sense of connection to their newborn.**

The act of being present, of holding space for the sacredness of birth, can create life-changing shifts in a father's sense of identity and purpose.

To all the dads walking alongside their partners — your love, your hands, your steady heart are so important. Your presence makes a world of difference.

You are part of this sacred journey, too. Your love matters to everyone.
